About Smart Hydration

Smart Hydration is an intelligent water bottle tracking system designed to help you maintain optimal hydration levels throughout the day. By providing regular reminders and real-time visual feedback, it makes staying hydrated easy and fun.

Why Hydration Matters

  • Boosts Energy: Proper hydration increases oxygen flow to your brain and muscles
  • Improves Focus: Studies show dehydration impacts cognitive performance
  • Better Health: Hydration supports kidney function and overall wellness
  • Better Skin: Adequate water intake promotes healthy skin from within
  • Aids Digestion: Water helps maintain proper digestive function

How to Use Smart Hydration

  1. Connect Device: Click "Connect Bluetooth" to pair your smart bottle
  2. Start Timer: Press "Start Alarm" to begin the 60-minute reminder cycle
  3. Watch Progress: See the water level animate as the timer counts down
  4. Drink Water: When the alarm goes off, drink a glass of water
  5. Reset: The bottle refills automatically and the cycle continues
  6. Stop Anytime: Use "Stop Alarm" to pause hydration reminders

Daily Hydration Goal

The general recommendation is to drink about 8 glasses (64 oz) of water per day, or about 2-3 liters. However, individual needs vary based on activity level, climate, and overall health. Smart Hydration helps you build this essential habit!
