About Your Smart Bottle

Welcome to the future of hydration! This smart bottle is designed to be your personal hydration coach. It tracks your water intake, reminds you to drink at regular intervals, and ensures you stay perfectly hydrated throughout the day.

Why It's Important

Staying hydrated is crucial for your health. Proper hydration improves energy levels, brain function, and physical performance. It can also prevent headaches and help your body's natural detoxification processes. Our smart bottle makes it easy and fun to meet your daily hydration goals.

How to Use

  1. Connect: Click the "Connect Bluetooth" button to pair the app with your smart bottle.
  2. Start: Press "Start Reminders" to begin receiving hourly alerts to drink water.
  3. Hydrate: When you get an alert, it's time to drink!
  4. Refill: Click "Fill Water" to see a cool animation and reset the water level in the app.
